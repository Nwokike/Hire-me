Our Collaboration Process

  1. 1. Discovery Call (30 min): A free, no-pressure chat to discuss your project, goals, and see if we're a good fit.
  2. 2. Proposal & Agreement: I'll send a detailed proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and investment.
  3. 3. Development Sprints: I build your project in stages, with regular check-ins for your feedback and approval.
  4. 4. Launch & Handover: We deploy the project, and I provide you with all necessary assets and training.
