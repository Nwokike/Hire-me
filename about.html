About Me & My Process

My Unique Background

My journey began in Agricultural & Bioresources Engineering. This foundation gives me a unique perspective on solving real-world problems, particularly those at the intersection of technology and sectors like agriculture.

I am now a self-driven Software Engineer, constantly learning and building practical solutions. I leverage technologies like Python, JavaScript, and AI to create applications that make a tangible impact. My experience includes building AI tools for farmers (YieldWise AI) and managing content-rich platforms (Igbo Archives).

I believe clear communication is key. Whether it's code or content, my goal is to deliver solutions that are not only functional but also understandable and maintainable.

My Project Process

1

Discovery & Quote

We start with a conversation to understand your goals, requirements, and budget. I'll then provide a detailed project proposal and quote.

2

Planning & Design

We agree on a timeline and milestones. I create wireframes or mockups for your approval before any code is written.

3

Development

I build your project using clean, modern code. I focus on responsiveness, performance, and best practices.

4

Review & Launch

You review the work, and I make any necessary revisions. Once approved, I help you deploy the final product.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a project take?

Timeline varies greatly. A basic website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex AI application could take 2-3 months or more. I'll provide a specific estimate during the discovery phase.

What's your payment process?

I typically require a 30-50% deposit to start, with the balance due upon completion and your approval. Payments can be made via bank transfer or secure online payment gateways.

Do you offer maintenance or updates?

Yes! I offer ongoing maintenance and update packages. We can discuss this after your initial project is launched.
